Refund & Returns Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive damaged or defective items, please:

  • Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damaged items
  • Contact us via WhatsApp at 0774899928

We will either replace the item or provide a full refund.

2. Wrong Items Delivered

If we deliver the wrong items:

  • Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery
  • Keep the incorrectly delivered items
  • We will deliver the correct items at no additional cost

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable food items (unless defective)
  • Personal care items that have been opened
  • Items returned after 24 hours

4. Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days
  • Refunds will be made using the original payment method
  • Cash refunds will be delivered with your next order
  • EcoCash refunds will be transferred to your EcoCash number

5. Order Cancellations

  • Orders can be cancelled before 7:00 PM on the day of ordering
  • Orders cancelled after 7:00 PM may incur a cancellation fee
  • Orders already prepared for delivery cannot be cancelled

6. Complaint Resolution

We are committed to resolving customer complaints fairly and promptly. If you have any issues with your order, please contact us immediately so we can make things right.

Delivery Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Delivery Area

We currently deliver to all areas within Harare, Zimbabwe. If you're unsure about your location, please contact us to confirm delivery availability.

2. Delivery Schedule

  • Orders placed by 7:00 PM are delivered the next day
  • Delivery hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Monday to Saturday
  • No Sunday deliveries (unless arranged in advance)
  • Public holidays may have modified delivery schedules

3. Delivery Fees

  • Orders $20-$29: $7 delivery fee
  • Orders $30-$49: $5 delivery fee
  • Orders $50+: Free delivery
  • Additional fees may apply for remote areas

4. Delivery Process

  • You will receive a WhatsApp message when your order is out for delivery
  • Our delivery personnel will call upon arrival
  • Please ensure someone is available to receive the order
  • Inspect items before signing for delivery

5. Failed Deliveries

If delivery fails due to:

  • No one available: $3 redelivery fee
  • Wrong address: $3 redelivery fee
  • Customer cancellation: Full delivery fee charged

6. Delivery Time Estimates

While we strive to deliver within the stated timeframe, delivery times may vary due to traffic, weather, or order volume. We will notify you of any significant delays.

7. Special Delivery Instructions

If you have special delivery instructions (e.g., leave at gate, call before arrival), please include them in the order notes. We will try to accommodate reasonable requests.

8. COVID-19 Safety Measures

  • Contactless delivery available upon request
  • Delivery personnel wear masks and practice hand hygiene
  • Please maintain social distancing during delivery
